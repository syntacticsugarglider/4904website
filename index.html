About

The First Robotics Competition is an international high school robotics competition.

In an annual event that begins every January, thousands of teams compete to design, fabricate, and program the systems of a robot over the course of six weeks. Teams of three robots from schools all over the country match up to conquer a new challenge, each with their own diverse strategy.

To inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
