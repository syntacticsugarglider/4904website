About

The First Robotics Competition is an international high school robotics competition.

In an annual event that begins every January, thousands of teams compete to design, fabricate, and program the systems of a robot over the course of six weeks. Teams of three robots from schools all over the world match up to conquer a new challenge, each with their own diverse strategy.

To inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
FIRST institutional mission

Team 4904, Bot Provoking, was founded in 2014 with the establishment of the Nueva High School in San Mateo, California. The Nueva School competes in annual FRC competitions in addition to off-season competitions such as CalGames and Chezy Champs. Team 4904 also carries out community STEAM outreach and mentors new members to maintain a strong roster (which currently comprises around 22% of the student body).
